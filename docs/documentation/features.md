# Features

Spanish Coach is packed with tools designed to make learning Spanish effective, engaging, and fun.

---

## Vocabulary Builder

Build a lasting vocabulary with our intelligent flashcard system.

- **Spaced Repetition** — Cards are shown at the optimal moment just before you'd forget them
- **Contextual examples** — Every word comes with example sentences so you learn words in context
- **Audio pronunciation** — Tap any word to hear it spoken by a native Spanish speaker
- **Personal word bank** — Save words from any lesson to review later
- **Progress tracking** — See exactly how many words you've learned and how many are due for review

---

## Grammar Lessons

Structured lessons that build your grammar knowledge step by step.

- Clear explanations in plain English
- Interactive exercises after every lesson
- Covers all key tenses: present, past (preterite & imperfect), future, subjunctive, and more
- Conjugation tables with audio for every verb
- Short lessons designed to fit into your day (5–10 minutes each)

---

## Conversation Practice

Real-world dialogues to build your speaking and listening confidence.

- **Guided dialogues** — Follow along and respond in realistic scenarios (ordering food, asking directions, making plans)
- **Listening exercises** — Train your ear with natural-speed Spanish audio
- **Speaking exercises** — Record yourself and compare with native speaker recordings
- **Scenario library** — Dozens of real-world conversation scenarios

---

## Practice Quizzes

Reinforce everything you've learned with varied quiz formats.

- Multiple choice
- Fill in the blank
- Translation (English → Spanish and Spanish → English)
- Listening comprehension
- Sentence construction

---

## Progress & Gamification

Stay motivated with streaks, achievements, and stats.

- **Daily streak** — Keep your streak alive by practicing every day
- **XP points** — Earn experience points for every activity completed
- **Achievements** — Unlock badges for milestones like "First 100 words" or "7-day streak"
- **Weekly summary** — A recap of what you learned each week
- **Leaderboard** — Friendly competition with friends (optional)

---

## Personalisation

Spanish Coach adapts to you.

- Set your daily study goal (5, 10, 15, or 20+ minutes)
- Choose your dialect preference (Latin American or Castilian Spanish)
- Light and dark mode support
- Offline mode — lessons and flashcards work without an internet connection
