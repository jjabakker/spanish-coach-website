## Common Issues

??? question "The app is crashing or won't open"
    1. Force-quit the app and reopen it.
    2. Check for an app update in the App Store.
    3. Restart your device.
    4. Reinstall the app — note that because data is stored locally, reinstalling will lose your answer history. Use as a last resort.

    If the problem continues, send a bug report from inside the app (just **shake your device** — a pre-filled email opens with a screenshot attached), or email us with your device model and iOS version.

??? question "Spanish speech sounds robotic"
    iOS ships with Standard Spanish voices by default — they sound mechanical. Download a free **Enhanced** or **Premium** Spanish voice from:

    **iOS Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices → Spanish**

    Pick a region (Spain, Mexico, Latin America, …) and tap the download icon next to any Enhanced or Premium voice. Back in Spanish Coach, tap the **gear icon → Speech** and select the voice you just downloaded.

??? question "I want to reset my progress"
    In the app, go to **Preferences → Data → Reset Progress**. You can reset verbs only, nouns only, or everything at once. Your word-selection filters are kept.

??? question "How do I send feedback or report a bug?"
    The fastest way is to **shake your device** from anywhere inside the app. A pre-filled email compose sheet opens immediately with a screenshot of the current screen attached. Describe what you saw and send.

## Contact Support

If you can't find what you're looking for, email us and we'll get back to you within **1–2 business days**.

**Email:** [support@spanishcoach.app](mailto:support@spanishcoach.app)

When emailing, please include:

- Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro)
- Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
- A brief description of the issue
- A screenshot if relevant
