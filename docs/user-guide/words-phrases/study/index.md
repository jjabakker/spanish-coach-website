# Study Words and Phrases

The Study screen for Words & Phrases is a browse-only reference: no testing, no answers recorded. Use it to look up an expression, refresh your memory before a session, or scan through what's in your current selection.

---

## What you see

A searchable list of every entry in your current selection, showing:

- The Spanish word or phrase
- The English (or Dutch) translation
- A small badge for the CEFR level
- A speaker icon — tap to hear the entry spoken aloud

Tap any entry to open its detail view, where you can see additional context including topic tags and (where relevant) usage notes.

---

## When to use Study vs Test

- **Study** is passive — read, listen, refresh. Nothing is recorded.
- [**Test**](../test/) is active — flashcards record your answers and feed the adaptive engine.

A common workflow is to spend a minute in Study scanning the set you're about to drill, then jump straight into the Test. That short warm-up makes the test stick faster than diving in cold.
