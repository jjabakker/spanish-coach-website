# Words & Phrases

The Words & Phrases section covers vocabulary that isn't a verb or a noun: **adjectives**, **adverbs**, **prepositions**, **numbers**, **greetings**, and **common everyday expressions**. Around 540 entries in total.

Unlike Verbs and Nouns, there is no separate Study section — Words & Phrases works in two steps: select your set, then drill it with flashcards.

---

## Setup

- [Select Words and Phrases](select/) — filter by CEFR level, frequency, and topic category (greetings, food, travel, numbers, …).

## Test

- [Test Words and Phrases](test/) — flashcards in either direction (Spanish ↔ your language), or a random mix.

---

## What kind of words are in here?

This is a mixed bag of everything that doesn't fit cleanly under verbs or nouns:

- **Adjectives** — masculine and feminine forms are merged into a single entry. The flashcard shows you the form appropriate to the gender.
- **Adverbs** — *siempre*, *nunca*, *despacio*, *bien*, *mal*, …
- **Numbers** — cardinal numbers, ordinals, fractions.
- **Greetings and social phrases** — *buenos días*, *mucho gusto*, *encantado* / *encantada*, …
- **Time and date expressions** — days, months, seasons, *en punto*, *hace falta*, …
- **Prepositions and conjunctions** — small but essential words: *para*, *por*, *aunque*, *sin embargo*, …

## How flashcards work

A word or phrase appears either in Spanish or in your native language depending on the direction you've chosen. Think about your answer, tap to reveal it, then rate yourself:

- **Correct** — you knew it without hesitation
- **Difficult** — you got there eventually, or were partially right
- **Incorrect** — you did not know it or were wrong

Each word appears exactly once per session regardless of how you rated it. The direction picker at the top of the screen lets you choose Spanish → your language, your language → Spanish, or a random mix.
