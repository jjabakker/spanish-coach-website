- **[Main Screen](main-screen/index.md)** — Your starting point. A quick tour of the home screen and how to navigate the app.

---

## Verbs Coach at a glance

The Verbs Coach has three areas:

**Setup** — Choose which verbs and tenses to work on before you start.

- [Selecting Verbs](verbs-coach/select-verbs/index.md) — Filter by CEFR level, frequency, and topic scenario.
- [Selecting Tenses](verbs-coach/select-tenses/index.md) — Toggle individual tenses on or off.

**Study** — Learn at your own pace with reference material.

- [Conjugation Tables](verbs-coach/conjugation-tables/index.md) — Full tables for every verb, irregular forms highlighted.
- [Word Meanings](verbs-coach/word-meanings-study/index.md) — Browse Spanish ↔ English translations.

**Tests** — Put your knowledge to the test.

- [Conjugation Drill](verbs-coach/conjugation-drill/index.md) — Type or pick the correct conjugated form.
- [Word Meanings Flashcard](verbs-coach/verb-meanings-test/index.md) — Flip cards to reveal translations.
- [Recognition](verbs-coach/recognition/index.md) — See a conjugated form; identify verb, tense, and person.
- [Gerund (Gerundio)](verbs-coach/gerundio/index.md) — Recall the gerund form of each verb.
- [Past Participle](verbs-coach/past-participle/index.md) — Recall the past participle of each verb.
- [Preterite (Indefinido)](verbs-coach/indefinido/index.md) — Recall the indefinido forms of each verb.
