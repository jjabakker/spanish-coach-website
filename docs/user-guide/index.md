# User Guide

Welcome to the Spanish Coach User Guide. Use the navigation on the left to explore each section of the app.

## What's in this guide

- **[Spanish Coach (Main Screen)](spanish-coach/)** — Your starting point. A quick tour of the home screen and how to navigate the app.

## Verbs Coach at a glance

The Verbs Coach has three areas:

**Setup** — Choose which verbs and tenses to work on before you start.

- [Selecting Verbs](verbs/select-verbs/) — Filter by CEFR level, frequency, topic scenario, or verb group.
- [Selecting Tenses](verbs/select-tenses/) — Toggle individual tenses on or off.

**Study** — Learn at your own pace with reference material.

- [Conjugation Tables](verbs/conjugation-tables/) — Full tables for every verb, irregular forms colour-coded.
- [Word Meanings](verbs/word-meanings-study/) — Browse Spanish ↔ your language translations.

**Tests** — Put your knowledge to the test.

- [Conjugation Drill](verbs/conjugation-drill/) — Type or pick the correct conjugated form.
- [Word Meanings Flashcard](verbs/verb-meanings-test/) — Flip cards to reveal translations.
- [Recognition](verbs/recognition/) — See a conjugated form; identify verb, tense, and person.
- [Gerund (Gerundio)](verbs/gerundio/) — Recall the gerund form of each verb.
- [Past Participle](verbs/past-participle/) — Recall the past participle of each verb.
- [Preterite (Indefinido)](verbs/indefinido/) — Recall the *yo* and *él* indefinido forms.

## Nouns Coach at a glance

The Nouns Coach has the same Setup / Study / Test layout.

**Setup** — [Select Nouns](nouns/setup-nouns/) by CEFR level, frequency, and topic.

**Study** — Reference material:

- [Meaning Study](nouns/meaning-study/) — searchable list with translations.
- [Alternate Gender Explained](nouns/alternate-gender/) — nouns whose meaning changes with *el* / *la*.
- [Noun Gender Rules](nouns/noun-gender/) — patterns for predicting gender from the ending.
- [Plural Formation](nouns/plural-formation/) — the -s / -es / -ces rules and accent shifts.

**Tests** — Active practice:

- [Meaning Test](nouns/meaning-test/) — flashcards for noun ↔ translation.
- [Pick the Correct Article](nouns/correct-article/) — *el* or *la*?
- [Singular / Plural](nouns/singular-plural/) — produce the plural form.
- [Alternate Gender Pairs](nouns/gender-pairs/) — drill nouns that change meaning with the article.
- [Recall Noun Gender](nouns/recall-gender/) — from the noun alone, recall masculine or feminine.
