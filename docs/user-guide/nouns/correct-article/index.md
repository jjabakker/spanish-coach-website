# Pick the Correct Article

The Article drill is a fast, focused test of one thing: **el** or **la**? For each noun in the session, you tap whichever article is correct. No typing, no flashcard flip — just a binary choice.

---

## How it works

A Spanish noun appears without an article. You tap **el** or **la**.

- Correct answer — the card advances after a short pause (or immediately if Auto-advance is off).
- Wrong answer — the correct article is shown, and you can study it for a moment before moving on.

Each noun appears once per session. Your answer history is recorded and feeds the adaptive engine — words you get wrong come back more often in future sessions.

---

## Why a dedicated article drill?

Article + noun is a unit in Spanish; the gender is part of the word, not a fact you derive afterwards. The Article drill is the fastest way to lock in gender for the nouns you already know the meaning of. It is the next step after [Meaning Test](../meaning-test/) feels comfortable.

For the small set of nouns whose **meaning** changes with the article — *el capital* vs *la capital*, etc. — use the [Alternate Gender Pairs](../gender-pairs/) drill instead.

---

## Tips

- Run this drill alongside [Meaning Test](../meaning-test/), not before. Producing the right article for a noun you don't yet know the meaning of has limited value.
- If you find yourself guessing more than answering, check the [Noun Gender Rules](../noun-gender/) study screen first — most nouns follow predictable patterns.
- The Article drill pairs well with topic-filtered selections — e.g. drilling article-correctness on **Food** vocabulary right after [Meaning Test](../meaning-test/) consolidates both meaning and gender for the same set.
